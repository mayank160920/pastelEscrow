* What is this project..?

This is a side project which I developed to safely escrow nfts (currently pastel ticket only).


* How does it work ..?

You can escrow an nft trade in 2 simple steps :

-> Step 1 : Place a Bid (Seller)

When you place a bid, 2 transactions are automatically sent sequentially:

Txn 1 : To get approval to transfer nft (nft contract)

Txn 2 : To place bid (escrow contract)

You will get a bid number after placing the bid successfully.

Send the bid number to the buyer.

-> Step 2 : Claim a Bid (Buyer)

Enter the bid number which you can get from the seller.

When you place a bid, 1 transaction is sent :

Txn 1 : To claim bid (escrow contract)

After claim is confirmed, the nft is send to the buyer and ftm is sent to the seller in the same transaction.


* Do I need to pay any fees..?

You just need to pay the transaction gas fees. Nothing more.


* Do I need to send my nft to smart contract...?

Nope, you don't need to send the nft to smart contract.

You just need to approve the smart contract to transfer nft on your behalf.


* Do you lock the nft..? Will I be able to transfer my nft after placing a bid..?

Nope, we don't lock your nft. It stays in your wallet.

You can also transfer it normally after placing the bid.


* Is it safe to use the contract..? Can I lose money ..?

Yup, it's safe to use the contract.

The contract neither stores funds or nfts untill the bid is claimed.

Also, if anything goes wrong during the nft/ftm transfer then the whole transaction is reverted.


* Can I trust this contract..?

It's just a fun side project.

I tried to make the contract simple and error free.

Also, there are no owner functions so even I can't control the contract.

You can check the contract code on ftmscan.
